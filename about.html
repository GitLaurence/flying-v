About Flying V

A story of Filipino resilience, quality, and service — since 1993.

Our History

Built on Filipino Grit

Flying V Petroleum Corporation was established in 1993 with a single goal: provide Filipinos with an affordable, reliable, and locally-owned alternative in the downstream petroleum industry.

Starting with a handful of stations in Metro Manila, we have grown to serve motorists across all major island groups — while remaining proudly independent and 100% Filipino.

Registered with the Department of Energy under the Oil Deregulation Law, Flying V operates under strict compliance, ensuring every liter you pump meets national quality standards. Our commitment to integrity and service has earned us the trust of millions of Filipinos over three decades.

1993

Founded

Incorporated and registered with the DOE as a downstream oil industry participant under the Oil Deregulation Law.

1998

Metro Manila Expansion

Expanded to 50 stations across Metro Manila, becoming a recognized brand in NCR.

2000

100 Stations

Reached the milestone of 100 stations nationwide, expanding beyond Metro Manila into nearby provinces.

2010

Visayas & Mindanao

Extended our network to Cebu, Iloilo, Davao, and Cagayan de Oro, serving all major island groups.

2024

300+ Stations

Now serving over one million customers per year across the Philippine archipelago.
